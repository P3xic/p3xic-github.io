Adding certificates to your LinkedIn profile can increase your chances of getting hired. Here's how:

  1. Go to your LinkedIn profile
  2. Scroll to the "Licenses & Certifications" section
  3. Click the "+" button to add a new certificate
  4. Fill in the course name, provider (e.g., Coursera), and issue date
  5. Include a link to the certificate if possible

Done! Now your profile stands out to recruiters looking for skilled professionals.
